What is an Appreciation Certificate?

An appreciation certificate is a formal document awarded to recognise achievements, contributions, dedication, outstanding performance or exceptional service. It acknowledges the efforts of individuals and serves as a tangible token of gratitude.

Appreciation certificates are widely used by companies for employee recognition programmes, non-profits for volunteer appreciation, schools for student achievements, and communities for contributor awards. They foster motivation and a positive organisational culture.
