Frequently Asked Questions

Is CertifyPro completely free?
Yes — 100% free. All templates, PDF downloads, PNG exports and logo uploads are free with no hidden charges, no watermarks and no account required.
Do I need to sign up or create an account?
No signup is required. Open the website, fill in your details and download your certificate. That's it.
Can I export certificates as PDF?
Yes. Certificates can be downloaded as print-ready PDF files (A4 landscape) and as PNG images suitable for sharing digitally.
Can I add my company logo?
Yes! Use the "Upload Logo" option to add your company or institution logo. The logo will appear on the certificate and is included in the exported PDF/PNG.
What certificate types are supported?
CertifyPro supports Internship Completion, Job Experience, Course Completion, Participation and Offer Letter certificates — with 7 unique templates to choose from.
Is my data private?
All certificate generation happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your information stays completely private.
